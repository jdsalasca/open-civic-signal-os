# Brand Kit

## Core Assets

- `assets/logo.svg`
- `assets/logo-mark.svg`
- `assets/logo-wordmark.svg`
- `assets/banner.svg`
- `assets/social-card.png`

## Usage Rules

- Maintain high contrast for accessibility.
- Do not distort icon proportions.
- Prefer dark civic blue background for hero cards.
- Keep subtitle under 12 words when used in social cards.

## Suggested Placements

- Repository social preview card.
- Docs landing hero image.
- Community updates and changelog announcements.
